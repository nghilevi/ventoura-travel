First, we utilize sessions to save your login information for future logins to the Application. Second, we utilize sessions to enable certain features of the Application, to better understand how you interact with the Application and to monitor aggregate usage by Ventoura Users and web traffic routing on the Application.

Third party advertisers on the Application may place or read cookies on your device. A cookie is a small data file that is transferred to your device for record-keeping purposes. You can instruct your device, by changing its options, to stop accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting a cookie from the websites you visit. If you do not accept cookies, however, you may not be able to use all portions of the Application or all functionality of the Application.

Third party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a Ventoura User's prior visits to Ventoura. You can disable these specific cookies that track prior visits for the sake of follow-up advertising by opting out at http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html
